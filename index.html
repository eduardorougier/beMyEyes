At Be My Eyes, our mission is to help people who are blind or have low vision by connecting them with compassionate and dedicated volunteers. We believe that everyone deserves to feel supported and empowered to handle everyday tasks independently. Our goal is to create an environment where every user feels valued and capable of overcoming the challenges they face with the help of others What We Offer:

  • Instant Help: Through our platform, users can connect with volunteers via live video calls. This allows them to receive quick assistance with everyday tasks like reading labels, identifying objects, or navigating their surroundings. The speed and efficiency of our help are key in fostering independence for our users.
  • A Global Community: With thousands of volunteers from all around the world, we’ve built a vibrant and supportive community. Our volunteers are passionate individuals eager to give their time and skills to help others. This cultural and geographic diversity enriches our community, fostering a sense of unity and empathy among all participants.
  • Easy-to-Use App: We take pride in offering an app that’s simple and intuitive. Every aspect of the app, from its design to its functionality, is crafted so that anyone—regardless of technical expertise—can easily request help or offer support. We want technology to be a bridge, not a barrier.

Creation of Be My Eyes

Be My Eyes is a free mobile app that helps make the world more accessible for blind and low-vision individuals. Launched in January 2015, the app connects users with over 6 million sighted volunteers worldwide through live video calls. Users can request help in more than 180 languages, making it the largest online community for blind and low-vision people. The idea for Be My Eyes came from Hans Jørgen Wiberg, a visually impaired furniture craftsman in Denmark. He realized that video calls could connect blind individuals with volunteers who could assist them with everyday tasks. The app quickly gained popularity, reaching over 10,000 users within 24 hours of its launch.

He created a revolutionary mobile app that connects users with volunteers through video calls. This innovative approach allows users to receive immediate assistance for various tasks, while volunteers can offer their support from anywhere in the world. Since its inception, Be My Eyes has rapidly grown, driven by a commitment to community and the belief that everyone deserves the opportunity to live independently.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make the world more accessible for blind and low-vision people. Through the power of technology and human connection, we aim to:

  • Promote independence for visually impaired individuals: Enabling users to navigate their world with greater confidence and ease.
  • Create a global community of helpers: Connecting those in need with volunteers who provide compassionate visual assistance.
  • Break down barriers and foster understanding: Building empathy and inclusivity through meaningful human connections.
  • Harness technology for social good: Innovating to ensure accessibility and support are always within reach.

How to Use Be My Eyes

1. Download the App

Get the Be My Eyes app from the App Store or Google Play. It's free and available to both users needing assistance and volunteers ready to help.

2. Register Your Profile

Sign up as either a visually impaired user or a volunteer. It only takes a minute, and it connects you to a global community instantly.

3. Connect and Start Helping

Users can request help with a single tap, while volunteers will be notified when someone needs visual assistance. You’ll connect through a live video call to provide or receive help in real time.
